A little bit about me

I grew up in a small town in Bulgaria, where my love for technology and math first started. I later left home to attend an international boarding school, living and learning alongside peers from over 80 countries. This experience gave me a global perspective and a passion for collaboration.

Now, I’m pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering at Brown, diving into hackathons, research projects, and hands-on learning with friends and peers. I’m always exploring new challenges, building projects, and sharpening my skills—both technical and creative.

When I’m not coding or tinkering with electronics, you can find me climbing a V3, experimenting with new cake recipies, or calling my cat.
